Volunteer Program

Volunteering for SDG 14 (Life Below Water) involves actively participating in marine conservation efforts, such as beach cleanups, coral reef restoration, sea turtle protection, and promoting sustainable fishing practices. By becoming a volunteer, you can help protect ocean ecosystems, reduce pollution, and contribute to the sustainable use of marine resources, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.
