Our Ocean Mission

The oceans cover more than 70% of our planet's surface and are home to an incredible diversity of life. Yet today, our oceans face unprecedented threats from pollution, overfishing, habitat destruction, and climate change. At Ocean Echoes, we're dedicated to SDG 14: Life Below Water - conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development.
